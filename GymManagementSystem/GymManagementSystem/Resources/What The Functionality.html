Technology partner program

Our technology partner program was founded to assist and empower start-up companies and SME's, with limited capital, in the development of software systems and the implementation of ICT systems.  The business model is designed to drastically reduce the cost of development, support and maintenance, building long term relationships with our partners, and allowing them to focus on their core business while we take care of all their ICT needs.

 
Software development

Our mission is to provide our clients and partners with innovative software solutions and top class services across all software disciplines.  From developing mobile apps to producing corporate enterprise systems for the private and public sectors, supporting, maintaining and upgrading your existing software, and integrating systems, we are well positioned to cater for your every need.  Our vast array of technical skills enables us to be a one-stop-shop for all your software requirements.

 
Hardware and networking

Through our ever-growing partner network we can source, supply and finance just about any hardware, whether it be servers, workstations, network devices, connectivity or consumables, no matter the brand.  Our team of IT professionals are experts in networking and information security, from setting up a new global network infrastructure to performing comprehensive security tests and ensuring that your systems are compliant with the ISO-27001 information security standard.

 
Intern program

What The Functionality is proud to introduce our software development internship program. The program aims to bridge the ‘digital divide’ by promoting access to real life ICT work experience which will contribute towards the reduction of unemployment in the ICT graduate space. The internship program provides candidates with the opportunity to start a career and advance their skills rapidly in a dynamic, innovative and fast paced environment.

 

Interactive and intuitive

 We deliver software that users love using!  The software is easy to use and requires little or no training. 

 

Cloud based

Access your system from anywhere, anytime, on any web enabled device.  Our software is fully cloud based and can run on your own server in your own data center, or in the cloud.

 

Fully integrated

All our software is fully integrated and can be integrated with any of your legacy systems on the fly and without any coding.

 

Cross platform

Our software operates on all operating systems including Windows, Android and Apple.  All our products come standard with mobile apps.
